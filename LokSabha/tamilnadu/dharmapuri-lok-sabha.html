Dharmapuri Lok Sabha

Dharmapuri Lok Sabha is one of the 39 Lok Sabha Constituencies in Tamil Nadu state.

The current MP, Dr. S. Senthilkumar won the seat in the 2019 Elections by a victory margin of 70,753 votes.
