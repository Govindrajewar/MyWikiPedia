Ramanathapuram Lok Sabha

Ramanathapuram Lok Sabha is one of the 39 Lok Sabha Constituencies in Tamil Nadu state.

The current MP, Kani K. Navas won the seat in the 2019 General Elections by a victory margin of 1,27,122 votes.
