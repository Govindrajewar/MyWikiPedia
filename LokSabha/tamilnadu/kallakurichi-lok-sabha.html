Kallakurichi Lok Sabha

Kallakurichi Lok Sabha is one of the 39 Lok Sabha Constituencies in Tamil Nadu state.

The current MP, Gautham Sigamani Pon won the seat in the 2019 Elections by a victory margin of 3,99,919 votes.
