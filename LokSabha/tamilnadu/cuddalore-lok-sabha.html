Cuddalore Lok Sabha

Cuddalore Lok Sabha is one of the 39 Lok Sabha Constituencies in Tamil Nadu state.

The current MP, T. R. V. S. Ramesh won the seat in the 2019 General Elections by a victory margin of 1,43,983 votes.
