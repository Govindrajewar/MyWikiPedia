Arani Lok Sabha

Arani Lok Sabha is one of the 39 Lok Sabha Constituencies in Tamil Nadu state.

The current MP, M. K. Vishnu Prasad won the seat in the 2019 Elections by a victory margin of 2,30,806 votes.
