Nilgiris Lok Sabha

Nilgiris Lok Sabha is one of the 39 Lok Sabha Constituencies in Tamil Nadu state.

The current MP, A. Raja won the seat in the 2019 General Elections by a victory margin of 2,05,823 votes.
