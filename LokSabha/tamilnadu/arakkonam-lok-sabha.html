Arakkonam Lok Sabha

Arakkonam Lok Sabha is one of the 39 Lok Sabha Constituencies in Tamil Nadu state.

The current MP, S. Jagathrakshakan won the seat in the 2019 Elections by a victory margin of 3,28,956 votes.
