Sriperumbudur Lok Sabha

Sriperumbudur Lok Sabha is one of the 39 Lok Sabha Constituencies in Tamil Nadu state.

The current MP, T. R. Baalu won the seat in the 2019 Elections by a victory margin of 5,07,955 votes.
