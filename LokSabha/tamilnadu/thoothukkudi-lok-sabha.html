Thoothukkudi Lok Sabha

Thoothukkudi Lok Sabha is one of the 39 Lok Sabha Constituencies in Tamil Nadu state.

The current MP, Kanimozhi Karunanidhi won the seat in the 2019 General Elections by a victory margin of 3,47,209 votes.
