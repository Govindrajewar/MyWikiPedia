Kancheepuram Lok Sabha

Kancheepuram Lok Sabha is one of the 39 Lok Sabha Constituencies in Tamil Nadu state.

The current MP, G. Selvam won the seat in the 2019 Elections by a victory margin of 2,86,632 votes.
