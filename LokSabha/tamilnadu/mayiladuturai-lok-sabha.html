Mayiladuthurai Lok Sabha

Mayiladuthurai Lok Sabha is one of the 39 Lok Sabha Constituencies in Tamil Nadu state.

The current MP, S. Ramalingam won the seat in the 2019 General Elections by a victory margin of 2,61,314 votes.
