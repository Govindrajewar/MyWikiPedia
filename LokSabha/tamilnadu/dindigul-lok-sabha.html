Dindigul Lok Sabha

Dindigul Lok Sabha is one of the 39 Lok Sabha Constituencies in Tamil Nadu state.

The current MP, P. Velusamy won the seat in the 2019 General Elections by a victory margin of 5,38,972 votes.
