Krishnagiri Lok Sabha

Krishnagiri Lok Sabha is one of the 39 Lok Sabha Constituencies in Tamil Nadu state.

The current MP, Dr. A. Chellakumar won the seat in the 2019 Elections by a victory margin of 1,56,765 votes.
