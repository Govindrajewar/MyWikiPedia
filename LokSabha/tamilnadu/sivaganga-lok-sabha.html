Sivaganga Lok Sabha

Sivaganga Lok Sabha is one of the 39 Lok Sabha Constituencies in Tamil Nadu state.

The current MP, Karti Chidambaram won the seat in the 2019 General Elections by a victory margin of 3,32,244 votes.
