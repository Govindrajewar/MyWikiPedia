Kanniyakumari Lok Sabha

Kanniyakumari Lok Sabha is one of the 39 Lok Sabha Constituencies in Tamil Nadu state.

The current MP, H. Vasanthakumar won the seat in the 2019 General Elections by a victory margin of 2,59,933 votes.
