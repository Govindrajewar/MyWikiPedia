Chidambaram Lok Sabha

Chidambaram Lok Sabha is one of the 39 Lok Sabha Constituencies in Tamil Nadu state.

The current MP, Thol. Thirumavalavan won the seat in the 2019 General Elections by a victory margin of 3,219 votes.
