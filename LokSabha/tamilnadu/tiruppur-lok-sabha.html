Tiruppur Lok Sabha

Tiruppur Lok Sabha is one of the 39 Lok Sabha Constituencies in Tamil Nadu state.

The current MP, K. Subbarayan won the seat in the 2019 General Elections by a victory margin of 93,368 votes.
