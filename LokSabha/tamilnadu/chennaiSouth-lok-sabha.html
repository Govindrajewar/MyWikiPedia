Chennai South Lok Sabha

Chennai South Lok Sabha is one of the 39 Lok Sabha constituencies in Tamil Nadu state.

The current MP, Thamizhachi Thangapandian won the seat in the 2019 Elections by a victory margin of 2,62,223 votes.
