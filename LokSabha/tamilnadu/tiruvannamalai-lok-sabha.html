Tiruvannamalai Lok Sabha

Tiruvannamalai Lok Sabha is one of the 39 Lok Sabha Constituencies in Tamil Nadu state.

The current MP, Annadurai C N won the seat in the 2019 Elections by a victory margin of 3,04,187 votes.
