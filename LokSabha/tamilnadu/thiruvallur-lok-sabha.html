Tiruvallur Lok Sabha

Tiruvallur Lok Sabha is one of the 39 Lok Sabha constituencies in Tamil Nadu state.

The current MP, Dr. K. Jeyakumar won the seat in the 2019 Elections by a victory margin of 3,56,955 votes.
