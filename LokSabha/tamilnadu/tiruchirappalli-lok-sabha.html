Tiruchirappalli Lok Sabha

Tiruchirappalli Lok Sabha is one of the 39 Lok Sabha Constituencies in Tamil Nadu state.

The current MP, Su. Thirunavukkarasar won the seat in the 2019 General Elections by a victory margin of 4,59,286 votes.
