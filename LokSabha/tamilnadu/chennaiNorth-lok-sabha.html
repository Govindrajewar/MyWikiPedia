Chennai North Lok Sabha

Chennai North Lok Sabha is one of the 39 Lok Sabha constituencies in Tamil Nadu state.

The current MP, Dr. Kalanidhi Veeraswamy won the seat in the 2019 Elections by a victory margin of 4,61,518 votes.
