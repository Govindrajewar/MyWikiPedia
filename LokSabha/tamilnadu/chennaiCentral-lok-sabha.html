Chennai Central Lok Sabha

Chennai Central Lok Sabha is one of the 39 Lok Sabha Constituencies in Tamil Nadu state.

The current MP, Dayanidhi Maran won the seat in the 2019 Elections by a victory margin of 3,01,520 votes.
