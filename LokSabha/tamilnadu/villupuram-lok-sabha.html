Viluppuram Lok Sabha

Viluppuram Lok Sabha is one of the 39 Lok Sabha Constituencies in Tamil Nadu state.

The current MP, D. Ravikumar won the seat in the 2019 Elections by a victory margin of 1,28,068 votes.
