Korba Lok Sabha

Korba Lok Sabha is one of the eleven Lok Sabha constituencies in Chhattisgarh state in central India.

The current MP, Jyotsna Charan Das Mahant won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 26,349 votes.
