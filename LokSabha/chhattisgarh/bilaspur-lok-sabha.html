Bilaspur Lok Sabha

Bilaspur Lok Sabha is one of the eleven Lok Sabha constituencies in Chhattisgarh state in central India.

The current MP, Arun Sao won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 1,41,763 votes.
