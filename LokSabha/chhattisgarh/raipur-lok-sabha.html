Raipur Lok Sabha

Raipur Lok Sabha is one of the eleven Lok Sabha constituencies in Chhattisgarh state in central India.

The current MP, Sunil Kumar Soni won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 3,48,238 votes.
