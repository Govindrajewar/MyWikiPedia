Janjgir-Champa Lok Sabha

Janjgir-Champa Lok Sabha is one of the eleven Lok Sabha constituencies in Chhattisgarh state in central India.

The current MP, Guharam Ajgalle won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 83,255 votes.
