Sarguja Lok Sabha

Sarguja Lok Sabha is one of the eleven Lok Sabha constituencies in Chhattisgarh state in central India.

The current MP, Renuka Singh won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 1,57,873 votes.
