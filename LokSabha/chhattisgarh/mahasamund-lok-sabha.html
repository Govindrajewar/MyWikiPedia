Mahasamund Lok Sabha

Mahasamund Lok Sabha is one of the eleven Lok Sabha constituencies in Chhattisgarh state in central India.

The current MP, Chunni Lal Sahu won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 90,511 votes.
