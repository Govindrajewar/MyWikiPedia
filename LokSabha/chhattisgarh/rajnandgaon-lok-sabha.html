Rajnandgaon Lok Sabha

Rajnandgaon Lok Sabha is one of the eleven Lok Sabha constituencies in Chhattisgarh state in central India.

The current MP, Santosh Pandey won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 1,11,966 votes.
