Nagaland Lok Sabha

Nagaland Lok Sabha constituency is the only Lok Sabha constituency in the Northeastern state of Nagaland.

The current MP, Tokheho Yepthomi won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 16,344 votes.
