Kolar Lok Sabha

Kolar Lok Sabha constituency is one of the 28 Lok Sabha constituencies in Karnataka state in southern India.

The current MP, S. Muniswamy won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 2,10,021 votes.
