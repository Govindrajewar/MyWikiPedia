Bangalore South Lok Sabha

Bangalore South Lok Sabha constituency is one of the 28 Lok Sabha constituencies in Karnataka state in southern India.

The current MP, Tejasvi Surya won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 3,31,192 votes.
