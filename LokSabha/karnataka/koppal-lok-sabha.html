Koppal Lok Sabha

Koppal Lok Sabha constituency is one of the 28 Lok Sabha constituencies in Karnataka state in southern India.

The current MP, Karadi Sanganna Amarappa won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 38,397 votes.
