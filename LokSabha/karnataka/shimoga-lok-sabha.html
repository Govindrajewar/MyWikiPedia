Shimoga Lok Sabha

Shimoga Lok Sabha constituency is one of the 28 Lok Sabha constituencies in Karnataka state in southern India.

The current MP, B. Y. Raghavendra won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 2,23,360 votes.
