Mandya Lok Sabha

Mandya Lok Sabha constituency is one of the 28 Lok Sabha constituencies in Karnataka state in southern India.

The current MP, Sumalatha Ambareesh won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 1,25,876 votes.
