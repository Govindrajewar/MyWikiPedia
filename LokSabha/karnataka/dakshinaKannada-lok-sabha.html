Dakshina Kannada Lok Sabha

Dakshina Kannada Lok Sabha constituency is one of the 28 Lok Sabha constituencies in Karnataka state in southern India.

The current MP, Nalin Kumar Kateel won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 2,74,621 votes.
