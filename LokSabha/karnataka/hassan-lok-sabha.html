Hassan Lok Sabha

Hassan Lok Sabha constituency is one of the 28 Lok Sabha constituencies in Karnataka state in southern India.

The current MP, Prajwal Revanna won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 1,41,224 votes.
