Uttara Kannada Lok Sabha

Uttara Kannada Lok Sabha constituency is one of the 28 Lok Sabha constituencies in Karnataka state in southern India.

The current MP, Anantkumar Hegde won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 4,79,649 votes.
