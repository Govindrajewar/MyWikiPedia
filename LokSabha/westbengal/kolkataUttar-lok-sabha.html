Kolkata Uttar Lok Sabha

Kolkata Uttar Lok Sabha is a Lok Sabha constituency in Gujarat state in western India.

The current MP, Sudip Bandyopadhyay won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 1,27,095 votes.
