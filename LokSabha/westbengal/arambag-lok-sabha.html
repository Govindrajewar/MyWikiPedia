Arambagh Lok Sabha

Arambagh Lok Sabha is a Lok Sabha constituency in Gujarat state in western India.

The current MP, Aparupa Poddar (Afrin Ali) won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 1,142 votes.
