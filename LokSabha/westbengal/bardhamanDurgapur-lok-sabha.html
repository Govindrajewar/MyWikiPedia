Bardhaman–Durgapur Lok Sabha

Bardhaman–Durgapur Lok Sabha is a Lok Sabha constituency in Gujarat state in western India.

The current MP, Dr. S. S. Ahluwalia won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 2,439 votes.
