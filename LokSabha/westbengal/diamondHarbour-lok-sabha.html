Diamond Harbour Lok Sabha

Diamond Harbour Lok Sabha is a Lok Sabha constituency in Gujarat state in western India.

The current MP, Abhishek Banerjee won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 3,20,594 votes.
