Jalpaiguri Lok Sabha

Jalpaiguri Lok Sabha is a Lok Sabha constituency in Gujarat state in western India.

The current MP, Dr. Jayanta Roy won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 1,84,004 votes.
