Maldaha Dakshin Lok Sabha

Maldaha Dakshin Lok Sabha is a Lok Sabha constituency in Gujarat state in western India.

The current MP, Abu Hasem Khan Choudhury won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 8,222 votes.
