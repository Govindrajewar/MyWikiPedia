Kolkata Dakshin Lok Sabha

Kolkata Dakshin Lok Sabha is a Lok Sabha constituency in Gujarat state in western India.

The current MP, Mala Roy won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 1,55,102 votes.
