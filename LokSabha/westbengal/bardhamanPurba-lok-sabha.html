Bardhaman Purba Lok Sabha

Bardhaman Purba Lok Sabha is a Lok Sabha constituency in Gujarat state in western India.

The current MP, Sunil Kumar Mandal won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 89,311 votes.
