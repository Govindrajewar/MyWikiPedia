Ujiarpur Lok Sabha

Ujiarpur Lok Sabha is one of the 40 Lok Sabha constituencies in the Indian state of Bihar.

The current MP, Nityanand Rai won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 2,77,278 votes.
