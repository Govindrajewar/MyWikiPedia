Hajipur Lok Sabha

Hajipur Lok Sabha is one of the 40 Lok Sabha constituencies in the Indian state of Bihar.

The current MP, Pashupati Kumar Paras won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 2,05,449 votes.
