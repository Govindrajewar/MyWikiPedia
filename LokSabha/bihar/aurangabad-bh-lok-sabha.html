Aurangabad Lok Sabha

Aurangabad Lok Sabha is one of the 40 Lok Sabha constituencies in the Indian state of Bihar.

The current MP, Sushil Kumar Singh won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 72,607 votes.
