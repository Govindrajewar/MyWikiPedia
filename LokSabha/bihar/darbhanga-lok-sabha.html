Darbhanga Lok Sabha

Darbhanga Lok Sabha is one of the 40 Lok Sabha constituencies in the Indian state of Bihar.

The current MP, Gopal Jee Thakur won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 2,67,979 votes.
