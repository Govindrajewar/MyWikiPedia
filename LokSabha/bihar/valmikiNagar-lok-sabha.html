Valmiki Nagar Lok Sabha

Valmiki Nagar is one of the 40 Lok Sabha constituencies in the Indian state of Bihar.

The current MP, Sunil Kumar won the seat in the 2021 Bye elections by a victory margin of 22,539 votes.
