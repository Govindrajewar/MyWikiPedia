Madhepura Lok Sabha

Madhepura Lok Sabha is one of the 40 Lok Sabha constituencies in the Indian state of Bihar.

The current MP, Dinesh Chandra Yadav won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 3,01,527 votes.
