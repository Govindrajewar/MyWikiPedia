Bhagalpur Lok Sabha

Bhagalpur Lok Sabha is one of the 40 Lok Sabha constituencies in the Indian state of Bihar.

The current MP, Ajay Kumar Mandal won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 2,77,630 votes.
