Sasaram Lok Sabha

Sasaram Lok Sabha is one of the 40 Lok Sabha constituencies in the Indian state of Bihar.

The current MP, Chhedi Paswan won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 1,65,745 votes.
