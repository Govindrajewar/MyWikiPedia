Gopalganj Lok Sabha

Gopalganj Lok Sabha is one of the 40 Lok Sabha constituencies in the Indian state of Bihar.

The current MP, Alok Kumar Suman won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 2,86,434 votes.
