Sitamarhi Lok Sabha

Sitamarhi is one of the 40 Lok Sabha constituencies in the Indian state of Bihar.

The current MP, Sunil Kumar Pintu won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 2,50,539 votes.
