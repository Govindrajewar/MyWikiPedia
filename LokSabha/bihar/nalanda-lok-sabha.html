Nalanda Lok Sabha

Nalanda Lok Sabha is one of the 40 Lok Sabha constituencies in the Indian state of Bihar.

The current MP, Kaushalendra Kumar won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 2,56,137 votes.
