Jamui Lok Sabha

Jamui Lok Sabha is one of the 40 Lok Sabha constituencies in the Indian state of Bihar.

The current MP, Chirag Kumar Paswan won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 2,41,049 votes.
