Purnia Lok Sabha

Purnia Lok Sabha is one of the 40 Lok Sabha constituencies in the Indian state of Bihar.

The current MP, Santosh Kumar Kushwaha won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 2,63,461 votes.
