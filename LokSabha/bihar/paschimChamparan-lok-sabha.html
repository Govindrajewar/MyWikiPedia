Paschim Champaran Lok Sabha

Paschim Champaran is one of the 40 Lok Sabha constituencies in the Indian state of Bihar.

The current MP, Dr. Sanjay Jaiswal won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 2,93,906 votes.
