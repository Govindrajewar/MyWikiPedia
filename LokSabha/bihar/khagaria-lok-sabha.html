Khagaria Lok Sabha

Khagaria Lok Sabha is one of the 40 Lok Sabha constituencies in the Indian state of Bihar.

The current MP, Mehboob Ali Kaiser won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 2,48,570 votes.
