Purvi Champaran Lok Sabha

Purvi Champaran is one of the 40 Lok Sabha constituencies in the Indian state of Bihar.

The current MP, Radha Mohan Singh won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 2,93,648 votes.
