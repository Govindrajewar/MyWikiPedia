Patna Sahib Lok Sabha

Patna Sahib Lok Sabha is one of the 40 Lok Sabha constituencies in the Indian state of Bihar.

The current MP, Ravi Shankar Prasad won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of Ravi Shankar Prasad votes.
