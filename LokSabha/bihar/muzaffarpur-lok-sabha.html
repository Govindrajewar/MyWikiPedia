Muzaffarpur Lok Sabha

Muzaffarpur Lok Sabha is one of the 40 Lok Sabha constituencies in the Indian state of Bihar.

The current MP, Ajay Nishad won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 4,09,988 votes.
