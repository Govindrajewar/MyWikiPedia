Buxar Lok Sabha

Buxar Lok Sabha is one of the 40 Lok Sabha constituencies in the Indian state of Bihar.

The current MP, Ashwani Kumar Choubey won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 1,17,609 votes.
