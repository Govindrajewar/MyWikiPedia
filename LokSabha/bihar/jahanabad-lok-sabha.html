Jahanabad Lok Sabha

Jahanabad Lok Sabha is one of the 40 Lok Sabha constituencies in the Indian state of Bihar.

The current MP, Chandeshwar Prasad won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 1,751 votes.
