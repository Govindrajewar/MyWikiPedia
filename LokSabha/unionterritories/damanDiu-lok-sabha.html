Daman and Diu Lok Sabha

Daman and Diu Lok Sabha constituency is one of the two Lok Sabha constituencies in the union territory of Dadra and Nagar Haveli and Daman and Diu in western India.

The current MP, Lalubhai Patel won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 9,942 votes.

Members of Parliament
