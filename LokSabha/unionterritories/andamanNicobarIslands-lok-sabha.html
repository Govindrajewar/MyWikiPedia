Andaman and Nicobar Islands Lok Sabha

Andaman and Nicobar Islands Lok Sabha constituency is the only Lok Sabha constituency in the Union Territory of Andaman and Nicobar Islands.

The current MP, Kuldeep Rai Sharma won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 1,407 votes.
