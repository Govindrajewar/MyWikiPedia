Dadra and Nagar Haveli Lok Sabha

Dadra and Nagar Haveli is one of the 21 Lok Sabha parliamentary constituencies in Odisha state in eastern India.

The current MP, Kalaben Delkar won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 51,269 votes.
