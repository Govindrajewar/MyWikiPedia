Chandigarh Lok Sabha

Chandigarh is a Lok Sabha parliamentary constituency and covers the entire Union Territory of Chandigarh.

The current MP, Kirron Anupam Kher won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 69,642 votes.
