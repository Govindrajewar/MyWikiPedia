Bhopal Lok Sabha

Bhopal Lok Sabha constituency is one of the 29 Lok Sabha constituencies in the Indian state of Madhya Pradesh.

The current MP, Sadhvi Pragya Singh Thakur won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 3,64,822 votes.
