Betul Lok Sabha

Betul Lok Sabha constituency is one of the 29 Lok Sabha constituencies in the Indian state of Madhya Pradesh.

The current MP, Durga Das Uikey won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 3,60,241 votes.
