Rewa Lok Sabha

Rewa Lok Sabha constituency is one of the 29 Lok Sabha constituencies in the Indian state of Madhya Pradesh.

The current MP, Janardan Mishra won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 3,12,807 votes.
