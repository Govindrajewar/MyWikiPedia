Balaghat Lok Sabha

Balaghat Lok Sabha constituency is one of the 29 Lok Sabha constituencies in the Indian state of Madhya Pradesh.

The current MP, Dhal Singh Bisen won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 2,42,066 votes.
