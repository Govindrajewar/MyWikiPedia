Tikamgarh Lok Sabha

Tikamgarh Lok Sabha constituency is one of the 29 Lok Sabha constituencies in the Indian state of Madhya Pradesh.

The current MP, Virendra Kumar Khatik won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 3,48,059 votes.
