Mandla Lok Sabha

Mandla Lok Sabha constituency is one of the 29 Lok Sabha constituencies in the Indian state of Madhya Pradesh.

The current MP, Faggan Singh Kulaste won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 97,674 votes.
