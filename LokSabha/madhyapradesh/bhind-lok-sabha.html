Bhind Lok Sabha

Bhind Lok Sabha constituency is one of the 29 Lok Sabha constituencies in the Indian state of Madhya Pradesh.

The current MP, Sandhya Rai won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 1,99,885 votes.
