Khargone Lok Sabha

Khargone Lok Sabha constituency is one of the 29 Lok Sabha constituencies in the Indian state of Madhya Pradesh.

The current MP, Gajendra Umrao Singh Patel won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 2,02,510 votes.
