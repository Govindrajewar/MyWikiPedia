Khandwa Lok Sabha

Khandwa Lok Sabha constituency is one of the 29 Lok Sabha constituencies in the Indian state of Madhya Pradesh.

The current MP, Gyaneswar Patil won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 82,140 votes.
