Khajuraho Lok Sabha

Khajuraho Lok Sabha constituency is one of the 29 Lok Sabha constituencies in the Indian state of Madhya Pradesh.

The current MP, V. D. Sharma won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 4,92,382 votes.
