Jabalpur Lok Sabha

Jabalpur Lok Sabha constituency is one of the 29 Lok Sabha constituencies in the Indian state of Madhya Pradesh.

The current MP, Rakesh Singh won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 4,54,744 votes.
