Damoh Lok Sabha

Damoh Lok Sabha constituency is one of the 29 Lok Sabha constituencies in the Indian state of Madhya Pradesh.

The current MP, Prahlad Patel won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 3,53,411 votes.
