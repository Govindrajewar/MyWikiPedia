Dhar Lok Sabha

Dhar Lok Sabha constituency is one of the 29 Lok Sabha constituencies in the Indian state of Madhya Pradesh.

The current MP, Chattar Singh Darbar won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 1,56,029 votes.
