Ratlam Lok Sabha

Ratlam Lok Sabha constituency is one of the 29 Lok Sabha constituencies in the Indian state of Madhya Pradesh.

The current MP, Guman Singh Damor won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 90,636 votes.
