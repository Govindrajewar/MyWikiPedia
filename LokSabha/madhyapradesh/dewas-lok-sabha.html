Dewas Lok Sabha

Dewas Lok Sabha constituency is one of the 29 Lok Sabha constituencies in the Indian state of Madhya Pradesh.

The current MP, Mahendra Solanki won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 3,72,249 votes.
