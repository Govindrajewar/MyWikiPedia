Hoshangabad Lok Sabha

Hoshangabad Lok Sabha constituency is one of the 29 Lok Sabha constituencies in the Indian state of Madhya Pradesh.

The current MP, Uday Pratap Singh won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 5,53,682 votes.
