Guna Lok Sabha

Guna Lok Sabha constituency is one of the 29 Lok Sabha constituencies in the Indian state of Madhya Pradesh.

The current MP, Krishna Pal Singh Yadav won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 1,25,549 votes.
