Indore Lok Sabha

Indore Lok Sabha constituency is one of the 29 Lok Sabha constituencies in the Indian state of Madhya Pradesh.

The current MP, Shankar Lalwani won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 5,47,754 votes.
