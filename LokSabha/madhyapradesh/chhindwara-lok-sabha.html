Chhindwara Lok Sabha

Chhindwara Lok Sabha constituency is one of the 29 Lok Sabha constituencies in the Indian state of Madhya Pradesh.

The current MP, Nakul Nath won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 37,536 votes.
