Narasaraopet Lok Sabha

Narasaraopet is one of the twenty-five lok sabha constituencies of Andhra Pradesh in India.

The current MP, Lavu Sri Krishna Devarayalu won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 1,53,978 votes.
