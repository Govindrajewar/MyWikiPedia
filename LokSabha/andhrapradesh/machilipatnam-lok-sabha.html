Machilipatnam Lok Sabha

Machilipatnam is one of the twenty-five lok sabha constituencies of Andhra Pradesh in India.

The current MP, Balashowry Vallabhaneni won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 60,141 votes.
