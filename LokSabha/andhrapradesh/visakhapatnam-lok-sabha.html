Visakhapatnam Lok Sabha

Visakhapatnam is one of the twenty-five lok sabha constituencies of Andhra Pradesh in India.

The current MP, M. V. V. Satyanarayana won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 4,414 votes.
