Srikakulam Lok Sabha

Srikakulam is one of the twenty-five lok sabha constituencies of Andhra Pradesh in India.

The current MP, Kinjarapu Ram Mohan Naidu won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 6,653 votes.
