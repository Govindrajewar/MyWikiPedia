Araku Lok Sabha

Araku is one of the twenty-five lok sabha constituencies of Andhra Pradesh in India.

This constituency was created on 19 February 2008 as a part of the implementation of the Presidential notification based on the recommendations of the Delimitation Commission of India.

The current MP, Goddeti Madhavi won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 2,24,089 votes.
