Anakapalli Lok Sabha

Anakapalli is one of the twenty-five lok sabha constituencies of Andhra Pradesh in India.

The current MP, Beesetti Venkata Satyavathi won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 89,192 votes.
