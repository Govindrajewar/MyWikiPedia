Tirupati Lok Sabha

Tirupati is one of the twenty-five lok sabha constituencies of Andhra Pradesh in India.

The current MP, Maddila Gurumoorthy won the seat in the 2021-Bypoll elections by a victory margin of 2,71,592 votes.
