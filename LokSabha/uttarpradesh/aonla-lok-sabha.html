Aonla Lok Sabha

Aonla Lok Sabha is one of the 80 Lok Sabha constituencies in the state of Uttar Pradesh.

The current MP, Dharmendra Kumar won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 1,13,743 votes.
