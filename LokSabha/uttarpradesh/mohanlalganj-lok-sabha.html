Mohanlalganj Lok Sabha

Mohanlalganj Lok Sabha is one of the 80 Lok Sabha constituencies in the state of Uttar Pradesh.

The current MP, Kaushal Kishore won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 90,204 votes.
