Bulandshahr Lok Sabha

Bulandshahr Lok Sabha is one of the 80 Lok Sabha constituencies in the state of Uttar Pradesh.

The current MP, Bhola Singh won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 2,90,057 votes.
