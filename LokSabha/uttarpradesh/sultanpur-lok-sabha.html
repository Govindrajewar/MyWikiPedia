Sultanpur Lok Sabha

Sultanpur Lok Sabha is one of the 80 Lok Sabha constituencies in the state of Uttar Pradesh.

The current MP, Maneka Gandhi won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 14,526 votes.
