Mainpuri Lok Sabha

Mainpuri Lok Sabha is one of the 80 Lok Sabha constituencies in the state of Uttar Pradesh.

The current MP, Dimple Yadav won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 2,88,461 votes.
