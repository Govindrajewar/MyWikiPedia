Varanasi Lok Sabha

Varanasi Lok Sabha is one of the 80 Lok Sabha constituencies in the state of Uttar Pradesh.

The current MP, Narendra Modi won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 4,79,505 votes.
