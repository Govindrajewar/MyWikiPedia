Saharanpur Lok Sabha

Saharanpur Lok Sabha is one of the 80 Lok Sabha constituencies in the state of Uttar Pradesh.

The current MP, Haji Fazlur Rehman won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 22,417 votes.
