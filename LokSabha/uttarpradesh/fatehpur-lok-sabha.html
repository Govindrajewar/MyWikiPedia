Fatehpur Lok Sabha

Fatehpur Lok Sabha is one of the 80 Lok Sabha constituencies in the state of Uttar Pradesh.

The current MP, Sadhvi Niranjan Jyoti won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 1,98,205 votes.
