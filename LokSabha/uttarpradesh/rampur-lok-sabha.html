Rampur Lok Sabha

Rampur Lok Sabha is one of the 80 Lok Sabha constituencies in the state of Uttar Pradesh.

The current MP, Ghanshyam Singh Lodhi won the seat in the 2022 Bye-elections by a victory margin of 42,192 votes.
