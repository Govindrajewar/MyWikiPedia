Farrukhabad Lok Sabha

Farrukhabad Lok Sabha is one of the 80 Lok Sabha constituencies in the state of Uttar Pradesh.

The current MP, Mukesh Rajput won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 2,21,702 votes.
