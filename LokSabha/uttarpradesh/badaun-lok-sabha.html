Badaun Lok Sabha

Badaun Lok Sabha is one of the 80 Lok Sabha constituencies in the state of Uttar Pradesh.

The current MP, Sanghmitra Maurya won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 18,454 votes.
