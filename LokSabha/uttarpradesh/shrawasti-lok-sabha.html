Shravasti Lok Sabha

Shravasti Lok Sabha is one of the 80 Lok Sabha constituencies in the state of Uttar Pradesh.

The current MP, Ram Shiromani Verma won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 5,320 votes.
