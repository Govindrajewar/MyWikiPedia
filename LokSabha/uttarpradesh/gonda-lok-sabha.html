Gonda Lok Sabha

Gonda Lok Sabha is one of the 80 Lok Sabha constituencies in the state of Uttar Pradesh.

The current MP, Kirti Vardhan Singh won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 1,66,360 votes.
