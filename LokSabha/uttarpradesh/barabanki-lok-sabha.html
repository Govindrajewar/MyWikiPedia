Barabanki Lok Sabha

Barabanki Lok Sabha is one of the 80 Lok Sabha constituencies in the state of Uttar Pradesh.

The current MP, Upendra Singh Rawat won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 1,09,970 votes.
