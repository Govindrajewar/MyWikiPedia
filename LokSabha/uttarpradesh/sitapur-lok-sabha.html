Sitapur Lok Sabha

Sitapur Lok Sabha is one of the 80 Lok Sabha constituencies in the state of Uttar Pradesh.

The current MP, Rajesh Verma won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 1,00,833 votes.
