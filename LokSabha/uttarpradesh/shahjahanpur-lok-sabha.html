Shahjahanpur Lok Sabha

Shahjahanpur Lok Sabha is one of the 80 Lok Sabha constituencies in the state of Uttar Pradesh.

The current MP, Arun Kumar Sagar won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 2,68,418 votes.
