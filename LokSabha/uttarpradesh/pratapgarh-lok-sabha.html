Pratapgarh Lok Sabha

Pratapgarh Lok Sabha is one of the 80 Lok Sabha constituencies in the state of Uttar Pradesh.

The current MP, Sangam Lal Gupta won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 1,17,752 votes.
