Mirzapur Lok Sabha

Mirzapur Lok Sabha is one of the 80 Lok Sabha constituencies in the state of Uttar Pradesh.

The current MP, Anupriya Singh Patel won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 2,32,008 votes.
