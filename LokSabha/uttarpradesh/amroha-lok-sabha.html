Amroha Lok Sabha

Amroha Lok Sabha is one of the 80 Lok Sabha constituencies in the state of Uttar Pradesh.

The current MP, Kunwar Danish Ali won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 63,248 votes.
