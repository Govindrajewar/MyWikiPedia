Salempur Lok Sabha

Salempur Lok Sabha is one of the 80 Lok Sabha constituencies in the state of Uttar Pradesh.

The current MP, Ravindra Kushwaha won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 1,12,615 votes.
