Azamgarh Lok Sabha

Azamgarh Lok Sabha is one of the 80 Lok Sabha constituencies in the state of Uttar Pradesh.

The current MP, Dinesh Lal Yadav "Nirahua" won the seat in the 2022 Bye-Elections by a victory margin of 8,679 votes.
