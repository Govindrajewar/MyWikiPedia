Baghpat Lok Sabha

Baghpat Lok Sabha is one of the 80 Lok Sabha constituencies in the state of Uttar Pradesh.

The current MP, Satya Pal Singh won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 23,502 votes.
