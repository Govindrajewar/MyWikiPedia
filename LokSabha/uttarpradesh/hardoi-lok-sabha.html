Hardoi Lok Sabha

Hardoi Lok Sabha is one of the 80 Lok Sabha constituencies in the state of Uttar Pradesh.

The current MP, Jai Prakash won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 1,32,474 votes.
