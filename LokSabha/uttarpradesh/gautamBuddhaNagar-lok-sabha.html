Gautam Buddha Nagar Lok Sabha

Gautam Buddha Nagar Lok Sabha is one of the 80 Lok Sabha constituencies in the state of Uttar Pradesh.

The current MP, Dr. Mahesh Sharma won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 3,36,922 votes.
