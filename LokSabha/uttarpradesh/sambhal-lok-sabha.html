Sambhal Lok Sabha

Sambhal Lok Sabha is one of the 80 Lok Sabha constituencies in the state of Uttar Pradesh.

The current MP, Shafiqur Rahman Barq won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 1,74,826 votes.
