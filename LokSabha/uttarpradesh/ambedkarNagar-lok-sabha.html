Ambedkar Nagar Lok Sabha

Ambedkar Nagar Lok Sabha is one of the 80 Lok Sabha constituencies in the state of Uttar Pradesh.

The current MP, Ritesh Pandey won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 95,880 votes.
