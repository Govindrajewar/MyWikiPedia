Maharajganj Lok Sabha

Maharajganj Lok Sabha is one of the 80 Lok Sabha constituencies in the state of Uttar Pradesh.

The current MP, Pankaj Chaudhary won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 3,40,424 votes.
