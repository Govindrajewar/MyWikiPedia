Bareilly Lok Sabha

Bareilly Lok Sabha is one of the 80 Lok Sabha constituencies in the state of Uttar Pradesh.

The current MP, Santosh Gangwar won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 1,67,282 votes.
