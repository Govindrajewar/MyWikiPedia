Jhansi Lok Sabha

Jhansi Lok Sabha is one of the 80 Lok Sabha constituencies in the state of Uttar Pradesh.

The current MP, Anurag Sharma won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 3,65,683 votes.
