Akbarpur Lok Sabha

Akbarpur Lok Sabha is one of the 80 Lok Sabha constituencies in the state of Uttar Pradesh.

The current MP, Devendra Singh won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 2,75,142 votes.
