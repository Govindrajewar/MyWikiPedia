Jaunpur Lok Sabha

Jaunpur Lok Sabha is one of the 80 Lok Sabha constituencies in the state of Uttar Pradesh.

The current MP, Shyam Singh Yadav won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 80,936 votes.
