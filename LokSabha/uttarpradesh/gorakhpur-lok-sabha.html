Gorakhpur Lok Sabha

Gorakhpur Lok Sabha is one of the 80 Lok Sabha constituencies in the state of Uttar Pradesh.

The current MP, Ravi Kishan won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 3,01,664 votes.
