Chandauli Lok Sabha

Chandauli Lok Sabha is one of the 80 Lok Sabha constituencies in the state of Uttar Pradesh.

The current MP, Mahendra Nath Pandey won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 13,959 votes.
