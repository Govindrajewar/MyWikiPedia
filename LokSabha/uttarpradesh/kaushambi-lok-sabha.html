Kaushambi Lok Sabha

Kaushambi Lok Sabha is one of the 80 Lok Sabha constituencies in the state of Uttar Pradesh.

The current MP, Vinod Sonkar won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 38,722 votes.
