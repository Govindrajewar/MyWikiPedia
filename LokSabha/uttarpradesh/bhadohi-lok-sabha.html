Bhadohi Lok Sabha

Bhadohi Lok Sabha is one of the 80 Lok Sabha constituencies in the state of Uttar Pradesh.

The current MP, Rameshchand Bind won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 43,615 votes.
