Domariyaganj Lok Sabha

Domariyaganj Lok Sabha is one of the 80 Lok Sabha constituencies in the state of Uttar Pradesh.

The current MP, Jagdambika Pal won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 1,05,321 votes.
