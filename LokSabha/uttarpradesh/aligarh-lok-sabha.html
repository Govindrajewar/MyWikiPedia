Aligarh Lok Sabha

Aligarh Lok Sabha is one of the 80 Lok Sabha constituencies in the state of Uttar Pradesh.

The current MP, Satish Kumar Gautam won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 2,29,261 votes.
