Unnao Lok Sabha

Unnao Lok Sabha is one of the 80 Lok Sabha constituencies in the state of Uttar Pradesh.

The current MP, Sakshi Maharaj won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 4,00,956 votes.
