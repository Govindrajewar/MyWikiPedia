Firozabad Lok Sabha

Firozabad Lok Sabha is one of the 80 Lok Sabha constituencies in the state of Uttar Pradesh.

The current MP, Chandrasen Jadon won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 28,781 votes.
