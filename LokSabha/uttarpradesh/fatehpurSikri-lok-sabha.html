Fatehpur Sikri Lok Sabha

Fatehpur Sikri Lok Sabha is one of the 80 Lok Sabha constituencies in the state of Uttar Pradesh.

The current MP, Rajkumar Chahar won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 4,95,065 votes.
