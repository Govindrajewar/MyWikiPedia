Bansgaon Lok Sabha

Bansgaon Lok Sabha is one of the 80 Lok Sabha constituencies in the state of Uttar Pradesh.

The current MP, Kamlesh Paswan won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 1,53,468 votes.
