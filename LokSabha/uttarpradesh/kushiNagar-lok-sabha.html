Kushinagar Lok Sabha

Kushinagar Lok Sabha is one of the 80 Lok Sabha constituencies in the state of Uttar Pradesh.

The current MP, Vijay Kumar Dubey won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 3,37,560 votes.
