Etawah Lok Sabha

Etawah Lok Sabha is one of the 80 Lok Sabha constituencies in the state of Uttar Pradesh.

The current MP, Ram Shankar Katheria won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 64,437 votes.
