Ghazipur Lok Sabha

Ghazipur Lok Sabha is one of the 80 Lok Sabha constituencies in the state of Uttar Pradesh.

The current MP, Afzal Ansari won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 1,19,392 votes.
