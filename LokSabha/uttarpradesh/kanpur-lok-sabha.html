Kanpur Lok Sabha

Kanpur Lok Sabha is one of the 80 Lok Sabha constituencies in the state of Uttar Pradesh.

The current MP, Satyadev Pachauri won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 1,55,934 votes.
