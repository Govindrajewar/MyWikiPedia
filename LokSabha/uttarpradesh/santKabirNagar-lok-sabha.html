Sant Kabir Nagar Lok Sabha

Sant Kabir Nagar Lok Sabha is one of the 80 Lok Sabha constituencies in the state of Uttar Pradesh.

The current MP, Praveen Kumar Nishad won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 35,749 votes.
