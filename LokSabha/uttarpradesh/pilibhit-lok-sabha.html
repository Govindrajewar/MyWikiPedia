Pilibhit Lok Sabha

Pilibhit Lok Sabha is one of the 80 Lok Sabha constituencies in the state of Uttar Pradesh.

The current MP, Varun Gandhi won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 2,55,627 votes.
