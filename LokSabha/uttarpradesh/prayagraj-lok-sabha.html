Allahabad Lok Sabha

Allahabad Lok Sabha is one of the 80 Lok Sabha constituencies in the state of Uttar Pradesh.

The current MP, Rita Bahuguna Joshi won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 1,84,275 votes.
