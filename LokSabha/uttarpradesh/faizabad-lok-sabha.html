Faizabad Lok Sabha

Faizabad Lok Sabha is one of the 80 Lok Sabha constituencies in the state of Uttar Pradesh. It includes Ayodhya city And Faizabad city.

The current MP, Lallu Singh won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 65,477 votes.
