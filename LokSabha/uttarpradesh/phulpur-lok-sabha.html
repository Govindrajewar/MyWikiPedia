Phulpur Lok Sabha

Phulpur Lok Sabha is one of the 80 Lok Sabha constituencies in the state of Uttar Pradesh.

The current MP, Keshari Devi Patel won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 1,71,968 votes.
