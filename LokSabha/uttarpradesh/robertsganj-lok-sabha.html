Robertsganj Lok Sabha

Robertsganj Lok Sabha is one of the 80 Lok Sabha constituencies in the state of Uttar Pradesh.

The current MP, Pakaudi Lal Kol won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 54,336 votes.
