Muzaffarnagar Lok Sabha

Muzaffarnagar Lok Sabha is one of the 80 Lok Sabha constituencies in the state of Uttar Pradesh.

The current MP, Dr. Sanjeev Kumar Balyan won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 4,01,150 votes.
