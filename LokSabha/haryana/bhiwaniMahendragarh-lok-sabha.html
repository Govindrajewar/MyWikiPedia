Bhiwani–Mahendragarh Lok Sabha

Bhiwani–Mahendragarh is one of the 10 Lok Sabha constituencies in the Haryana state in northern India.

The current MP, Dharambir Singh won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 4,44,463 votes.
