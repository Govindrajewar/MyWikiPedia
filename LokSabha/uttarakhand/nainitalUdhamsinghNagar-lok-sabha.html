Nainital–Udhamsingh Nagar Lok Sabha

Nainital–Udhamsingh Nagar Lok Sabha is one of the five Lok Sabha constituencies in Uttarakhand.

The current MP, Ajay Bhatt won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 3,39,096 votes.
