Tehri Garhwal Lok Sabha

Tehri Garhwal Lok Sabha is one of the five Lok Sabha constituencies in Uttarakhand.

The current MP, Mala Rajya Laxmi Shah won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 3,00,586 votes.
