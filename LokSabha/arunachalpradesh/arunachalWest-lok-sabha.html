Arunachal West Lok Sabha

Arunachal West Lok Sabha is one of the two Lok Sabha constituencies in Arunachal Pradesh state in northeastern India.

The current MP, Kiren Rijiju won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 1,74,843 votes.
