Arunachal East Lok Sabha

Arunachal East Lok Sabha is one of the two Lok Sabha constituencies in Arunachal Pradesh state in northeastern India.

The current MP, Tapir Gao won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 69,948 votes.
