Thiruvananthapuram Lok Sabha

Thiruvananthapuram is one of the 20 Lok Sabha constituencies in Kerala state in southern India.

The current MP, Dr. Shashi Tharoor won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 99,989 votes.
