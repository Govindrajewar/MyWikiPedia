Malappuram Lok Sabha

Malappuram is one of the 20 Lok Sabha constituencies in Kerala state in southern India.

The current MP, M. P. Abdussamad Samadani won the seat in the 2021 (By-Polls) elections by a victory margin of 1,14,615 votes.
