Wayanad Lok Sabha

Wayanad is one of the 20 Lok Sabha constituencies in Kerala state in southern India.

This constituency was created on 19 February 2008 as a part of the implementation of the Presidential notification based on the recommendations of the Delimitation Commission of India.

The current MP, Rahul Gandhi won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 4,31,770 votes.
