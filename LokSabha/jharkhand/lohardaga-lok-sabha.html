Lohardaga Lok Sabha

Lohardaga Lok Sabha is one of the 14 Lok Sabha constituencies in Jharkhand state in eastern India.

The current MP, Sudarshan Bhagat won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 10,363 votes.
