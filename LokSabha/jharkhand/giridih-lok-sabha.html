Giridih Lok Sabha

Giridih Lok Sabha is one of the 14 Lok Sabha constituencies in Jharkhand state in eastern India.

The current MP, Chandra Prakash Choudhary won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 2,48,347 votes.
