Singhbhum Lok Sabha

Singhbhum Lok Sabha is one of the 14 Lok Sabha constituencies in Jharkhand state in eastern India.

The current MP, Geeta Koda won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 72,155 votes.
