Chatra Lok Sabha

Chatra Lok Sabha is one of the 14 Lok Sabha constituencies in Jharkhand state in eastern India.

The current MP, Sunil Kumar Singh won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 3,77,871 votes.
