Rajmahal Lok Sabha

Rajmahal Lok Sabha is one of the 14 Lok Sabha constituencies in Jharkhand state in eastern India.

The current MP, Vijay Kumar Hansdak won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 99,195 votes.
