Kodarma Lok Sabha

Kodarma Lok Sabha is one of the 14 Lok Sabha constituencies in Jharkhand state in eastern India.

The current MP, Annapurna Devi Yadav won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 4,55,600 votes.
