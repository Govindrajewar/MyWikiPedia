Hazaribagh Lok Sabha

Hazaribagh Lok Sabha is one of the 14 Lok Sabha constituencies in Jharkhand state in eastern India.

The current MP, Jayant Sinha won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 4,79,548 votes.
