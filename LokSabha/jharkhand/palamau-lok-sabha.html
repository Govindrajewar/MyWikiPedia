Palamau Lok Sabha

Palamau Lok Sabha is one of the 14 Lok Sabha constituencies in Jharkhand state in eastern India.

The current MP, Vishnu Dayal Ram won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 4,77,606 votes.
