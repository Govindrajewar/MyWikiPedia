Jamshedpur Lok Sabha

Jamshedpur Lok Sabha is one of the 14 Lok Sabha constituencies in Jharkhand state in eastern India.

The current MP, Bidyut Baran Mahato won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 3,02,090 votes.
