Sikkim Lok Sabha

Sikkim Lok Sabha constituency is a Lok Sabha constituency which covers the entire area of the state of Sikkim.

The current MP, Indra Hang Subba won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 12,433 votes.
