Baramulla Lok Sabha

Baramulla Lok Sabha is one of the five Lok Sabha constituencies in Jammu and Kashmir in northern India.

The current MP, Mohammad Akbar Lone won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 30,053 votes.

Assembly Segments

The Legislative Assembly of Jammu and Kashmir was dissolved by the Governor on 21 November 2018.
