Jammu Lok Sabha

Jammu Lok Sabha is one of the five Lok Sabha constituencies in Jammu and Kashmir in northern India.

The current MP, Jugal Kishore Sharma won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 3,02,875 votes.

Assembly Segments

The assembly is currently dissolved.
