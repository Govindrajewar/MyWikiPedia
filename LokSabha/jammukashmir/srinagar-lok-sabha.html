Srinagar Lok Sabha

Srinagar Lok Sabha is one of the five Lok Sabha constituencies in Jammu and Kashmir in northern India.

The current MP, Farooq Abdullah won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 70,050 votes.

Assembly Segments

The assembly is currently dissolved.
