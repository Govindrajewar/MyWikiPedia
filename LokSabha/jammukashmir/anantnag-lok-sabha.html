Anantnag Lok Sabha

Anantnag Lok Sabha is one of the five Lok Sabha constituencies in Jammu and Kashmir in northern India.

The current MP, Hasnain Masoodi won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 6,676 votes.

Assembly Segments

The Legislative Assembly of Jammu and Kashmir was dissolved by the Governor on 21 November 2018.
