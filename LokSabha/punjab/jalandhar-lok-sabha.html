Jalandhar Lok Sabha

Jalandhar Lok Sabha is one of the 13 Lok Sabha constituencies in Punjab state in northern India.

The current MP, Sushil Kumar Rinku won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 58,691 votes.
