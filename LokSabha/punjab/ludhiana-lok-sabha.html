Ludhiana Lok Sabha

Ludhiana Lok Sabha is one of the 13 Lok Sabha constituencies in Punjab state in northern India.

The current MP, Ravneet Singh Bittu won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 76,732 votes.
