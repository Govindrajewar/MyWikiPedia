Anandpur Sahib Lok Sabha

Anandpur Sahib Lok Sabha is one of the 13 Lok Sabha constituencies in Punjab state in northern India.

The current MP, Manish Tewari won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 46,884 votes.
