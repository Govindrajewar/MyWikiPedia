Hoshiarpur Lok Sabha

Hoshiarpur Lok Sabha is one of the 13 Lok Sabha constituencies in Punjab state in northern India.

The current MP, Som Parkash won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 48,530 votes.
