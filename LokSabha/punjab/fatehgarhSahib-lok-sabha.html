Fatehgarh Sahib Lok Sabha

Fatehgarh Sahib Lok Sabha is one of the 13 Lok Sabha constituencies in Punjab state in northern India.

The current MP, Dr. Amar Singh won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 93,898 votes.
