Bathinda Lok Sabha

Bathinda Lok Sabha is one of the 13 Lok Sabha constituencies in Punjab state in northern India.

The current MP, Harsimrat Kaur Badal won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 21,772 votes.
