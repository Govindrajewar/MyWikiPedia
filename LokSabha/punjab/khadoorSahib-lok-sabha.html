Khadoor Sahib Lok Sabha

Khadoor Sahib Lok Sabha is one of the 13 Lok Sabha constituencies in Punjab state in northern India.

The current MP, Jasbir Singh Gill won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 1,40,573 votes.
