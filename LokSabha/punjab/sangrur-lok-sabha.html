Sangrur Lok Sabha

Sangrur Lok Sabha is one of the 13 Lok Sabha constituencies in Punjab state in northern India.

The current MP, Simranjit Singh Mann won the seat in the 2022 Bye Elections by a victory margin of 6,245 votes.
