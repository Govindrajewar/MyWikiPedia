Firozpur Lok Sabha

Firozpur Lok Sabha is one of the 13 Lok Sabha constituencies in Punjab state in northern India.

The current MP, Sukhbir Singh Badal won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 1,98,850 votes.
