Shimla Lok Sabha

Shimla Lok Sabha is one of the four Lok Sabha constituencies in Himachal Pradesh state in northern India.

The current MP, Suresh Kumar Kashyap won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 3,27,515 votes.
