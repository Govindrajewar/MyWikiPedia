Mandi Lok Sabha

Mandi Lok Sabha is one of the four Lok Sabha constituencies in Himachal Pradesh state in northern India.

The current MP, Pratibha Singh won the seat in the 2021 Bye elections by a victory margin of 8,766 votes.
