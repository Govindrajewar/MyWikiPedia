Hamirpur Lok Sabha

Hamirpur Lok Sabha is one of the four Lok Sabha constituencies in Himachal Pradesh state in northern India.

The current MP, Anurag Singh Thakur won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 3,99,572 votes.
