Kangra Lok Sabha

Kangra Lok Sabha is one of the four Lok Sabha constituencies in Himachal Pradesh state in northern India.

The current MP, Kishan Kapoor won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 4,77,623 votes.
