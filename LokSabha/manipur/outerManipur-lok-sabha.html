Outer Manipur Lok Sabha

Outer Manipur Lok Sabha is one of the two Lok Sabha constituencies in Manipur, a state in northeastern India.

The current MP, Lorho S. Pfoze won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 73,782 votes.
