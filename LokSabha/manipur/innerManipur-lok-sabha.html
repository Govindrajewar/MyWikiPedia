Inner Manipur Lok Sabha

Inner Manipur Lok Sabha is one of the two Lok Sabha constituencies in Manipur, a state in northeastern India.

The current MP, R.K. Ranjan Singh won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 17,755 votes.
