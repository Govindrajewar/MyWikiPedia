Mumbai North Lok Sabha

Mumbai North Lok Sabha constituency is one of the 48 Lok Sabha constituencies of Maharashtra state in western India.

The current MP, Gopal Shetty won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 4,65,247 votes.

Assembly Segments

Presently, Mumbai North Lok Sabha constituency comprises six Vidhan Sabha segments.
