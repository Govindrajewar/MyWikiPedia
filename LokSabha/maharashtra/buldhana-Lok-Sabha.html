Buldhana Lok Sabha

Buldhana Lok Sabha constituency is one of the 48 Lok Sabha constituencies of Maharashtra state in western India.

Assembly Segments

Presently, Buldhana Lok Sabha constituency comprises six Vidhan Sabha segments. These segments are:
