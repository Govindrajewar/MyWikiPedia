Mumbai North East Lok Sabha

Mumbai North East Lok Sabha constituency is one of the 48 Lok Sabha constituencies of Maharashtra state in western India.

The current MP, Manoj Kishorbhai Kotak won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 2,26,486 votes.
