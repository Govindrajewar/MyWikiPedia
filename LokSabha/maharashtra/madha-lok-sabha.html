Madha Lok Sabha

Madha Lok Sabha constituency is one of the 48 Lok Sabha constituencies of Maharashtra state in western India.

This constituency was created on 19 February 2008 as a part of the implementation of the Presidential notification based on the recommendations of the Delimitation Commission of India.

The current MP, Ranjitsinh Naik-Nimbalkar won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 85,764 votes.
