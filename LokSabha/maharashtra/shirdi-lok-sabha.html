Shirdi Lok Sabha

Shirdi Lok Sabha constituency is one of the 48 Lok Sabha constituencies of Maharashtra state in western India.

This constituency was created on 19 February 2008 as a part of the implementation of the Presidential notification based on the recommendations of the Delimitation Commission of India.

The current MP, Sadashiv Lokhande won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 1,20,195 votes.
