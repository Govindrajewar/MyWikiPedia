Bhandara–Gondiya Lok Sabha

Bhandara–Gondiya Lok Sabha constituency is one of the 48 Lok Sabha (parliamentary) constituencies of Maharashtra state in western India.

Assembly Segments

Presently, Bhandara–Gondiya Lok Sabha constituency comprises six Vidhan Sabha segments.
