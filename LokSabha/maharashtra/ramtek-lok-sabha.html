Ramtek Lok Sabha

Ramtek Lok Sabha constituency is one of the 48 Lok Sabha constituencies of Maharashtra state in western India.

The current MP, Krupal Tumane won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 1,26,783 votes.

Assembly Segments

Presently, Ramtek Lok Sabha constituency comprises six Vidhan Sabha segments.
