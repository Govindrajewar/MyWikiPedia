Satara Lok Sabha

Satara Lok Sabha constituency is one of the 48 Lok Sabha constituencies of Maharashtra state in western India.

The current MP, Shriniwas Patil won the seat in the 2019 (Bye) elections by a victory margin of 87,717 votes.

Assembly Segments

Presently, Satara Lok Sabha constituency comprises six Vidhan Sabha segments.
