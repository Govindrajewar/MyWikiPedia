Dhule Lok Sabha

Dhule Lok Sabha constituency is one of the 48 Lok Sabha constituencies of Maharashtra state in western India.

The current MP, Dr. Subhash Bhamre won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 2,29,243 votes.
