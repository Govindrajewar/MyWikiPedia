Chandrapur Lok Sabha

Chandrapur is one of the 48 Lok Sabha constituencies of Maharashtra state in western India.

The current MP, Suresh Dhanorkar won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 44,763 votes.

Assembly Segments

Presently, Chandrapur Lok Sabha constituency comprises six Vidhan Sabha segments. These segments are:
