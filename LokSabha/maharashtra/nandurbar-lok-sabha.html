Nandurbar Lok Sabha

Nandurbar Lok Sabha constituency is one of the 48 Lok Sabha constituencies of Maharashtra state in western India.

The current MP, Dr. Heena Vijaykumar Gavit won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 95,629 votes.

Assembly Segments

Presently, Nandurbar Lok Sabha constituency comprises six Vidhan Sabha segments.
