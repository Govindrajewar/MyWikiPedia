Thane Lok Sabha

Thane Lok Sabha constituency is one of the 48 Lok Sabha constituencies of Maharashtra state in western India.

The current MP, Rajan Vichare won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 4,12,145 votes.

Assembly Segments

Presently, Thane Lok Sabha constituency comprises six Vidhan Sabha segments.
