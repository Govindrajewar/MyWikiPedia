Solapur Lok Sabha

Solapur Lok Sabha constituency is one of the 48 Lok Sabha constituencies of Maharashtra state in western India.

The current MP, Jaisidhesvar Swami won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 1,58,608 votes.
