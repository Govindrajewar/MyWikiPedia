Jalna Lok Sabha

Jalna Lok Sabha constituency is one of the 48 Lok Sabha constituencies in Maharashtra state in Western India.

The current MP, Raosaheb Danve won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 3,32,815 votes.

Assembly Segments

Presently, Jalna Lok Sabha constituency comprises six Vidhan Sabha segments.
