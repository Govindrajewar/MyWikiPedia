Hingoli Lok Sabha

Hingoli Lok Sabha constituency is one of the 48 Lok Sabha constituencies in Maharashtra state in western India.

The current MP, Hemant Patil won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 2,77,856 votes.
