Beed Lok Sabha

Beed Lok Sabha constituency is one of the 48 parliamentary constituencies of the Lok Sabha in the state of Maharashtra.

The current MP, Pritam Munde won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 1,68,368 votes.
