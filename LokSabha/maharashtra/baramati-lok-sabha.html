Baramati Lok Sabha

Baramati Lok Sabha constituency is one of the 48 Lok Sabha constituencies in Maharashtra state in western India.

Assembly Segments

Presently, Baramati Lok Sabha constituency comprises six Vidhan Sabha segments. These segments are:
