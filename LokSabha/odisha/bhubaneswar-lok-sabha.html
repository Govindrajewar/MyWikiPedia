Bhubaneswar Lok Sabha

Bhubaneswar is one of the 21 Lok Sabha parliamentary constituencies in Odisha state in eastern India.

The current MP, Aparajita Sarangi won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 23,839 votes.
