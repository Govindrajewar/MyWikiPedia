Mayurbhanj Lok Sabha

Mayurbhanj is one of the 21 Lok Sabha parliamentary constituencies in Odisha state in eastern India.

The current MP, Bishweswar Tudu won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 25,256 votes.
