Cuttack Lok Sabha

Cuttack is one of the 21 Lok Sabha parliamentary constituencies in Odisha state in eastern India.

The current MP, Bhartruhari Mahtab won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 1,21,201 votes.
