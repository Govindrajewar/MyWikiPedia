Dhenkanal Lok Sabha

Dhenkanal is one of the 21 Lok Sabha parliamentary constituencies in Odisha state in eastern India.

The current MP, Mahesh Sahoo won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 35,412 votes.
