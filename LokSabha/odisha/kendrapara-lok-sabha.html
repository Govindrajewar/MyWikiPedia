Kendrapara Lok Sabha

Kendrapara is one of the 21 Lok Sabha parliamentary constituencies in Odisha state in eastern India.

The current MP, Anubhav Mohanty won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 1,52,584 votes.
