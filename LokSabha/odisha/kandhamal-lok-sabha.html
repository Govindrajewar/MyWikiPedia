Kandhamal Lok Sabha

Kandhamal is one of the 21 Lok Sabha parliamentary constituencies in Odisha state in eastern India.

This constituency was created on 19 February 2008 as a part of the implementation of the Presidential notification based on the recommendations of the Delimitation Commission of India.

The current MP, Achyutananda Samanta won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 1,49,216 votes.
