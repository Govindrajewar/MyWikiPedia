Bolangir Lok Sabha

Bolangir is one of the 21 Lok Sabha parliamentary constituencies in Odisha state in eastern India.

The current MP, Sangeeta Kumari Singh Deo won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 19,516 votes.
