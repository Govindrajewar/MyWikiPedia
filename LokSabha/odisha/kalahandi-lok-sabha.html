Kalahandi Lok Sabha

Kalahandi is one of the 21 Lok Sabha parliamentary constituencies in Odisha state in eastern India.

The current MP, Basanta Kumar Panda won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 26,814 votes.
