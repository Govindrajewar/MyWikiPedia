Nabarangpur Lok Sabha

Nabarangpur is one of the 21 Lok Sabha parliamentary constituencies in Odisha state in eastern India.

The current MP, Ramesh Chandra Majhi won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 41,634 votes.
