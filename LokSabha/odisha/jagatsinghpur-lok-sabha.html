Jagatsinghpur Lok Sabha

Jagatsinghpur is one of the 21 Lok Sabha parliamentary constituencies in Odisha state in eastern India.

The current MP, Rajashree Mallick won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 2,71,655 votes.
