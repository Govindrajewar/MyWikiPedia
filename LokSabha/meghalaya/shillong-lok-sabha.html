Shillong Lok Sabha

Shillong Lok Sabha constituency is one of the two Lok Sabha constituencies in Meghalaya state in northeastern India.

The current MP, Vincent H. Pala won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 1,52,433 votes.
