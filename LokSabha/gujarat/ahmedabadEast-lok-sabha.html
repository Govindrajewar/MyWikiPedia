Ahmedabad East Lok Sabha

Ahmedabad East Lok Sabha is a Lok Sabha constituency in Gujarat state in western India.

The current MP, Hasmukhbhai Somabhai Patel won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 4,34,330 votes.
