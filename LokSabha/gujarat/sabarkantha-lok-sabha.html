Sabarkantha Lok Sabha

Sabarkantha Lok Sabha is a Lok Sabha constituency in Gujarat state in western India.

The current MP, Dipsinh Shankarsinh Rathod won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 2,68,987 votes.
