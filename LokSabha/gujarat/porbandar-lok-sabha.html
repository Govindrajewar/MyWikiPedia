Porbandar Lok Sabha

Porbandar Lok Sabha is a Lok Sabha constituency in Gujarat state in western India.

The current MP, Rameshbhai Lavjibhai Dhaduk won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 2,29,823 votes.
