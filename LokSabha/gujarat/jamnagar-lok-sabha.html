Jamnagar Lok Sabha

Jamnagar Lok Sabha is a Lok Sabha constituency in Gujarat state in western India.

The current MP, Poonamben Hematbhai Maadam won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 2,36,804 votes.
