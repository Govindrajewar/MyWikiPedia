Ahmedabad West Lok Sabha

Ahmedabad West Lok Sabha is a Lok Sabha constituency in Gujarat state in western India.

The current MP, Kirit Premjibhai Solanki won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 3,21,546 votes.
