Dahod Lok Sabha

Dahod Lok Sabha is a Lok Sabha constituency in Gujarat state in western India.

The current MP, Jasvantsinh Sumanbhai Bhabhor won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 1,27,596 votes.
