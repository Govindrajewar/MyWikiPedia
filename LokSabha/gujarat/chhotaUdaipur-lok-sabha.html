Chhota Udaipur Lok Sabha

Chhota Udaipur Lok Sabha is a Lok Sabha constituency in Gujarat state in western India.

The current MP, Gitaben Rathva won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 3,77,943 votes.
