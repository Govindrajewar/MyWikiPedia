Vadodara Lok Sabha

Vadodara Lok Sabha is a Lok Sabha constituency in Gujarat state in western India.

The current MP, Ranjanben Dhananjay Bhatt won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 5,89,177 votes.
