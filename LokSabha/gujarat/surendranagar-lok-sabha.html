Surendranagar Lok Sabha

Surendranagar Lok Sabha is a Lok Sabha constituency in Gujarat state in western India.

The current MP, Dr. Mahendra Munjapara won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 2,77,437 votes.
