Bhilwara Lok Sabha

Bhilwara Lok Sabha is one of the 25 Lok Sabha constituencies in Rajasthan state in western India.

The current MP, Subhash Chandra Baheria won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 6,11,460 votes.
