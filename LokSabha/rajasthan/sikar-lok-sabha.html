Sikar Lok Sabha

Sikar Lok Sabha is one of the 25 Lok Sabha constituencies in Rajasthan state in western India.

The current MP, Swami Sumedhanand Saraswati won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 2,97,156 votes.
