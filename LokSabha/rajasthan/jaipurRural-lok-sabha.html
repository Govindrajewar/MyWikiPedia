Jaipur Rural Lok Sabha

Jaipur Rural Lok Sabha is one of the 25 Lok Sabha constituencies in Rajasthan state in western India.

The current MP, Col. Rajyavardhan Singh Rathore won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 3,93,171 votes.
