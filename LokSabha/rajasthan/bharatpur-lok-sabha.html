Bharatpur Lok Sabha

Bharatpur Lok Sabha is one of the 25 Lok Sabha constituencies in Rajasthan state in western India.

The current MP, Ranjeeta Koli won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 3,18,399 votes.
