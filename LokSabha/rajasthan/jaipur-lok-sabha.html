Jaipur Lok Sabha

Jaipur Lok Sabha is one of the 25 Lok Sabha constituencies in Rajasthan state in western India.

The current MP, Ramcharan Bohra won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 4,30,626 votes.
