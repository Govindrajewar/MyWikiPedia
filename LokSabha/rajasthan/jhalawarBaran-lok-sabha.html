Jhalawar-Baran Lok Sabha

Jhalawar-Baran Lok Sabha is one of the 25 Lok Sabha constituencies in Rajasthan state in western India.

The current MP, Dushyant Singh won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 4,53,928 votes.
