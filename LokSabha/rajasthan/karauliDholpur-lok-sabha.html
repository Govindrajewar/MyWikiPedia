Karauli-Dholpur Lok Sabha

Karauli-Dholpur Lok Sabha is one of the 25 Lok Sabha constituencies in Rajasthan state in western India.

The current MP, Manoj Rajoria won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 97,682 votes.
