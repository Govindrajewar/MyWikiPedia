Jodhpur Lok Sabha

Jodhpur Lok Sabha is one of the 25 Lok Sabha constituencies in Rajasthan state in western India.

The current MP, Gajendra Singh Shekhawat won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 2,74,440 votes.
