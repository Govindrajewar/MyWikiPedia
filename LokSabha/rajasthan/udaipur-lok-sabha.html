Udaipur Lok Sabha

Udaipur Lok Sabha is one of the 25 Lok Sabha constituencies in Rajasthan state in western India.

The current MP, Arjunlal Meena won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 4,37,914 votes.
