Ajmer Lok Sabha

Ajmer Lok Sabha is one of the 25 Lok Sabha constituencies in Rajasthan state in western India.

The current MP, Bhagirath Choudhary won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 4,16,424 votes.
