Rajsamand Lok Sabha

Rajsamand Lok Sabha is one of the 25 Lok Sabha constituencies in Rajasthan state in western India.

The current MP, Diya Kumari won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 5,51,916 votes.
