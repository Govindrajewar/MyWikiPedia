Nagaur Lok Sabha

Nagaur Lok Sabha is one of the 25 Lok Sabha constituencies in Rajasthan state in western India.

The current MP, Hanuman Beniwal won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 1,81,260 votes.
