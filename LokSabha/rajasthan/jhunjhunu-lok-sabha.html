Jhunjhunu Lok Sabha

Jhunjhunu Lok Sabha is one of the 25 Lok Sabha constituencies in Rajasthan state in western India.

The current MP, Narendra Kumar won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 3,02,547 votes.
