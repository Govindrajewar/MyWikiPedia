Bikaner Lok Sabha

Bikaner Lok Sabha is one of the 25 Lok Sabha constituencies in Rajasthan state in western India.

The current MP, Arjun Ram Meghwal won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 2,64,081 votes.
