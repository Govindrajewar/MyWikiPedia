Mahabubabad Lok Sabha

Mahabubabad Lok Sabha is one of the 17 Lok Sabha constituencies in the state of Telangana.

The current MP, Kavitha Malothu won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 1,46,663 votes.
