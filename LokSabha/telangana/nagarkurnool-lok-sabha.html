Nagarkurnool Lok Sabha

Nagarkurnool Lok Sabha is one of the 17 Lok Sabha constituencies in the state of Telangana.

The current MP, Pothuganti Ramulu won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 1,89,748 votes.
