Bhongir Lok Sabha

Bhongir Lok Sabha is one of the 17 Lok Sabha constituencies in the state of Telangana.

The current MP, Komatireddy Venkat Reddy won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 5,119 votes.
