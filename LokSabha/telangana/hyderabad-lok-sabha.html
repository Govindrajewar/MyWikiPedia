Hyderabad Lok Sabha

Hyderabad Lok Sabha is one of the 17 Lok Sabha constituencies in the state of Telangana.

The current MP, Asaduddin Owaisi won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 2,82,186 votes.
