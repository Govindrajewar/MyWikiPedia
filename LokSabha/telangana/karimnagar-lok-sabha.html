Karimnagar Lok Sabha

Karimnagar Lok Sabha is one of the 17 Lok Sabha constituencies in the state of Telangana.

The current MP, Bandi Sanjay Kumar won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 89,508 votes.
