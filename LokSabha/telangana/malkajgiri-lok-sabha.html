Malkajgiri Lok Sabha

Malkajgiri Lok Sabha is one of the 17 Lok Sabha constituencies in the state of Telangana.

The current MP, Anumula Revanth Reddy won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 10,919 votes.
