Peddapalli Lok Sabha

Peddapalli Lok Sabha is one of the 17 Lok Sabha constituencies in the state of Telangana.

The current MP, Venkatesh Netha Borlakunta won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 95,180 votes.
