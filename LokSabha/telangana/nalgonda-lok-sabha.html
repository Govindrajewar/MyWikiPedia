Nalgonda Lok Sabha

Nalgonda Lok Sabha is one of the 17 Lok Sabha constituencies in the state of Telangana.

The current MP, Nalamada Uttam Kumar Reddy won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 25,682 votes.
