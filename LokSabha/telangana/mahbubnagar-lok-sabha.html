Mahabubnagar Lok Sabha

Mahabubnagar Lok Sabha is one of the 17 Lok Sabha constituencies in the state of Telangana.

The current MP, Manne Srinivas Reddy won the seat in the 2019 elections by a victory margin of 77,829 votes.
